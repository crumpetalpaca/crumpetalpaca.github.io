The Kiev 30

2025-07-22

The second love of my life

I got the Kiev 30 for Christmas last year and it was love at first sight.

Me and father dearest got into (and in his case back into) 16mm photography with a Minolta-16 P and he decided to gift me an hold Kiev 30 in pretty much mint condition.

The Kiev 30 is a pretty blatant copy of the original Minolta-16 although it has a single feature wich blows the Minolta-16 OUT OF THE WATER

Manual Focus

Althought this seems quite well ... the baisic feature which is taken for granted on almost every camera most of the sub-miniature cameras that Minolta produced do not have a manual focus (with the exception of the Minolta MG) whith most of the cameras having a fixed focus, and the original Minolta-16 not even focusing to infininity and requiring an expesive and now rare lense to be usable for lanscape photograpy.!

One of my favorite parts of the camera is the view finder as it quite litterall is just a piece of glass with no magnification just kinda placed at the front of the camera :3, so it just kinda serves mors of an asthetic purpose
